About us

Greely Hot Pizzeria was established in 1992, and it’s been a family run business ever since. Led by the two brothers with over 30 years of experience in the pizzeria business, Greely Hot Pizzeria continues to serve the customers with the same delicious pies. Everything served is made from scratch. Our pizza features the freshest ingredients, and our famous pizza sauce.
We also serve Italian dishes, appetizers, and salads.
