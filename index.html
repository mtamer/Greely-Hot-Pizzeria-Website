Welcome To Greely Hot Pizzeria

Welcome to Greely Hot Pizzeria's website. We are a family owned and operated business that has been serving Greely and the surrounding communities for over 20 years. If you are one of our many loyal customers, we thank you for your patronage over the years. And if you have not visited our pizza shop or tried our delicious pizzas, we would like to welcome you to come in, meet our family, and enjoy a slice. The food we serve to our customers is made fresh daily. From the dough that we make each morning, to our delicious sauces, and fresh cut ingredients, the food we serve our customers is the food we serve our own family. Not only do our families serve this great community, but Greely is also our home, and we take great pride in that.
